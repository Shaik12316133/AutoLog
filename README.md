# 🚗 AutoLog – AI-Powered Vehicle Management & Maintenance Intelligence

AutoLog is an advanced vehicle management and maintenance automation system built with n8n, Groq AI models, and intelligent workflow automation.
It helps vehicle owners track maintenance, analyze vehicle health, manage costs, automate reminders, and interact with an AI-powered automotive assistant — all from a single workflow engine.

Designed for Indian vehicle owners and fleet users, AutoLog delivers smart insights, predictive maintenance alerts, service history tracking, AI analytics, and automated email reporting.

* 🔧 Vehicle profile & ownership management
* 🛠️ Service history logging with cost tracking

## ⚡ Workflow Modules

### 1. Vehicle Setup & Profile Management

* Save vehicle information
* Store owner details
* Track mileage, fuel type, engine, transmission, and purchase data
* Automatically send welcome/setup emails

### 2. Service Record Automation

* Log maintenance & repair services
* Calculate:

  * Parts cost
  * Labour cost
  * Total service cost
  * Cost per KM
* Generate automated service summary emails

### 3. AI Vehicle Analytics

Powered by Groq LLMs:

* Vehicle health scoring
* Cost efficiency analysis
* Maintenance pattern detection
* Recurring issue identification
* Resale value estimation
* Smart recommendations & action plans

### 4. AI Chat Assistant

An intelligent automotive assistant that:

* Understands vehicle history
* Answers maintenance questions
* Provides repair advice
* Suggests service actions
* Estimates Indian market repair costs

### 5. Reminder & Maintenance Engine

* Oil change monitoring
* Upcoming service alerts
* Mileage-based reminders
* Date-based reminders
* Urgent maintenance notifications

### 6. Manual Sync & AI Reporting

Generate complete AI vehicle reports including:

* Health score
* Cost analysis
* Maintenance summary
* Financial efficiency
* Depreciation insights
* Priority action items
